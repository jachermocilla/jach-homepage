  • Aim

    • This course will give students an overview of smart cards and related technologies. This course is made possible in collaboration with Advanced Card Systems Limited, the leading provider of smart card readers and solutions.

  • Objectives

    • At the end of this course the students should be able to:

    1. understand the operation of smart cards; and
    2. develop software solutions using smart cards

  • Topics

    1. Overview of the Smart Card Industry
      1. History and Development
      2. Current State of Smart Card Technologies
      3. Future Technology Trends
      4. Why Implement a Smart Card System
      5. Relative Merits of Smart Cards vs. Alternative Technolgies
    2. Smart Cards
      1. Contact Cards
      2. Contactless Cards
      3. Contact and Contactless Interoperability
      4. Related Industry Specifications
    3. Smart Card Readers
      1. Contact Cards
      2. Contactless Cards
      3. Contact and Contactless Interoperability
      4. Related Industry Specifications
    4. Smart Card Applications
      1. Components of Smart Card Systems
      2. Card Life Cycle Management Architecture
      3. Typical Financial Application (E-Purse)
      4. Loyalty Card Application
      5. Multi-application Card
    5. Related Technologies
      1. Public Key Infrastructure
      2. Biometrics
