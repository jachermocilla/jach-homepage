  • Aim

    • The aim of this course is to introduce students to the basic research methods in Computer Science.

  • Objectives

    • At the end of this course the students should be able to:

    1. read, analyze, and evaluate published research papers in Computer Science;
    2. write a paper with quality suitable for submission in conferences or journals;
    3. orally present a research paper.

  • Evaluation/Grading (Tentative)

    • The student will get a grade of S if the following criteria are satisfied.

    • Student is always present in class.
    • Student has submitted a final paper based on topic assigned.
    • Student has orally presented the topic assigned.
    Otherwise, a grade of U will be given.
